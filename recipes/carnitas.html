Carnitas

Description

These carnitas capture that elusive combination of flavourful, juicy AND crispiness. Pan frying to get the golden bits is not optional! Broiling/grilling will not produce the same results.

Ingredients

  • 4 lb pork shoulder
  • 2 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place pork in a slow cooker with all ingredients.
  2. Slow cook on low for 10 hours or on high for 7 hours.
  3. Shred using two forks.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a large non stick pan or well seasoned skillet over high heat. Spread pork in the pan, drizzle over some juices. Wait until the juices evaporate and the bottom side is golden brown and crusty. Turn and just briefly sear the other side – you don’t want to make it brown all over because then it’s too crispy, need tender juicy bits.
  5. Remove pork from skillet. Repeat in batches.
  6. Just before serving, drizzle over more juices and serve hot.
